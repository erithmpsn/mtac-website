What We Do

Core Services

A contractor network built for rapid, reliable deployment across Birmingham and Montgomery — with the documentation municipal clients require.

Nuisance Abatement

Municipal code-compliant abatement for overgrown lots, hazardous structures, and ordinance violations. Fully documented for city records.

Lot Clearing & Brush Removal

Full-site clearing of overgrowth, brush, and vegetation for vacant lots, pre-development parcels, and municipal right-of-way.

Junk Vehicle Removal

Coordination and removal of abandoned or inoperable vehicles from public and private property, including towing logistics.

Junk & Debris Removal

Bulk haul-away of construction debris, household junk, and post-demolition material from residential and commercial sites.

Vacant Property Cleanouts

Interior and exterior cleanouts for vacant buildings, abandoned homes, and distressed properties ready for resale or redevelopment.

Community Beautification

Targeted revitalization projects for neighborhoods, community organizations, and local government beautification initiatives.
